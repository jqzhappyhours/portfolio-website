Pole Shape Classifier

A Streamlit app that analyzes pole dance videos and labels each second with the performer's current shape, using MediaPipe pose landmarks and a gradient boosting classifier. Recognizes six shapes — airwalk, climb, inside leg hang, invert, outside leg hang, and pencil — with an overall F1 score of 0.89.

  • Python
  • MediaPipe
  • scikit-learn
  • Streamlit
